Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why choose Surya Car Rentals?

Nomad Wheels offers a wide range of well-maintained cars at affordable prices. Our commitment to customer satisfaction and excellent service makes us a trusted choice for car rentals in Rajahmundry.

2. Is there a limit on kilometers?

No, we offer unlimited kilometers on all our rentals. You can drive as much as you need without worrying about extra charges.

3. What types of cars are available for rent?

We offer a variety of cars including luxury, budget, and SUVs to suit all your travel needs.

4. How do I book a car?

You can book a car through our website or by contacting our customer service team.

5. What documents are required to rent a car?

You need to provide a valid driving license, ID proof, and address proof to rent a car.

6. Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period by contacting our customer service team.

7. Are there any hidden charges?

No, we believe in transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

8. What is the fuel policy?

Our cars are provided with a full tank of fuel. You are required to return the car with a full tank.

Yes, the minimum rental period is 24 hours.

10. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.

11. Do you offer roadside assistance?

Yes, we offer 24/7 roadside assistance for all our rentals.

12. Can I choose a specific car model?

Yes, you can choose a specific car model based on availability.

13. Are there any age restrictions for renting a car?

Yes, you must be at least 21 years old to rent a car from us.

14. Do you provide child seats?

Yes, child seats are available upon request.

15. Can I rent a car for outstation travel?

Yes, our cars can be rented for both local and outstation travel.

16. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment methods.

17. Is insurance included in the rental?

Yes, all our rentals include basic insurance coverage.

18. Can I add an additional driver?

Yes, you can add an additional driver by providing their driving license and ID proof.

19. What happens in case of an accident?

In case of an accident, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will assist you with the necessary steps.

20. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via phone, email, or through our website's contact form.
